How Vontobel Works

Vontobel demonstrates modern payment workflows using secure server-side order creation and gateway-handled authorization. When a transaction is initiated, a unique order reference is generated and forwarded to the payment partner for processing. The gateway manages authentication, validation, and confirmation, returning a verified status. Vontobel records this status for transparency and reporting purposes only. No sensitive payment data is stored on the platform, ensuring compliance with security standards and regulatory expectations.
